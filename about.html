ABOUT YENUYOO FARMS

Registered in May 2016, Yenuyoo farms is into the production of seed rice and seed maize at the moment: it however intends to go into the production of other crops like soya beans, cowpea, groundnut and vegetable seed production in future.The rice field is located in a community called Wantugu in the Tolon District of the Northern Region. Currently,Yenuyoo farms has 400 hectares (1000acres) of land for the production of seed rice and seed maize. It has permanently engaged 6 people but engages more people when the need arises.

The farm is basically into two main activities:

  • It grows rice seeds for the seed market (sold to farmers to produce paddy rice)
  • It also produces paddy rice for the consumer market.

Yenuyoo farms was also registered in July 2017 to go into distribution and retail of fertilizer. It has 3 outlets, one in Tamale (Vittin), Nyankpala (Tolon) and another one in Dimabi (Tolon).
