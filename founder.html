FOUNDER - ISAAC YENUYOO GBOGLU

EARLY LIFE AND EDUCATION

After his Secondary School education from 2006 to 2009 at T.I Ahmadiyya Senior High School (Kumasi), He went further to study Bachelor of Science in Renewable Natural Resource Management (Forestry and forest resources management) from 2010-2014 at the University for Development Studies (U.D.S, Nyankpala campus).In May 2015, he went for Training on Rice Seed Production in St Louis, Senegal. Recognizing the importance of I.T in today’s world of business, he acquired a Professional Certificate inMicrosoft Office (MS. Word, Excel, power point and outlook) and the Internet at the National Institute of Information Technology (NIIT) from December 2016 - March 2017.

HIS WORKING EXPLOITS

Isaac Yenuyoo served the Nation at the Council for Scientific and Industrial Research, Savanna Agricultural Research Institute (CSIR - SARI) from September 2014 - August 2015. He worked with Heritage Seeds Company Limited (Tamale) as the company’s Rice Seed Technician from May 2015 to March 2017.As the Rice Seed Technician, he was responsible for monitoring the cultivation and all related activities of rice production for the company. He also managed the company’s rice out growers (farmer groups).

ESTABLISHMENTS

He owns a farm by name Yenuyoo Farms. He manages the cultivation and all production activities of his enterprise. He also takes care of the marketing and sales of the farm produce. He is also a fertilizer distributor and retailer. Currently, he as 3 stores: Tamale, Nyankpala and Dimabi (Tolon).

MEET THE FOUNDER

Isaac Yenuyoo Gboglu is a Ghanaian business person. Born on the 24 April 1990 in Tamale, the Northern Regional capital of Ghana. He is the founder and managing director of Yenuyoo Farms and Yenuyoo Agro.
